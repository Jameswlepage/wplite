What
We Do.

— Process · 工程

Four movements,
one intention.

聴

01 · Listen

We begin with conversation. How do you live? What should a room feel like at 6am? We map the emotional architecture before we draw a single line.

絞

02 · Distill

We reduce the brief to its essential qualities — not a list of features but a set of principles that will guide every decision from floor plan to door handle.

組

03 · Compose

Materials, light, and proportion are composed into space. We prototype physical samples and refine until every element earns its place.

作

04 · Realize

We oversee fabrication and installation with the same precision we bring to design. The built space matches the one we envisioned together.
