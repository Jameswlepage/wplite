Philosophy

What guides us

Kanso - Simplicity

Eliminate what does not serve. Every element should earn its presence in the room. Complexity is not sophistication; clarity is.

Shizen - Naturalness

Materials should be honest. Wood should feel like wood. Stone should carry weight. We never disguise a material as something it is not.

Ma - Negative Space

The space between objects matters as much as the objects themselves. We design the emptiness, the pauses that give a room its rhythm.

Hygge - Comfort

A space should hold you. Warmth is texture, light quality, seat height, and the softness underfoot.

Wabi-sabi - Imperfection

We honor patina, grain, and the marks of making. Time improves what we build.

Lagom - Balance

Not too much, not too little. Every proportion is considered, from the width of a threshold to the weight of a handle.
