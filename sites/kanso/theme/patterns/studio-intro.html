Emi Nakamura

Emi founded Kanso in 2019 after a decade practicing between Tokyo and Copenhagen. Trained at Waseda and the Royal Danish Academy, her work sits between the Japanese instinct for spatial poetry and the Swiss insistence on rigor. She leads a small studio of architects, material specialists, and craftspeople who share the belief that less, done well, is always enough.
