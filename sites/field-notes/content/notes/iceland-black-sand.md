The beach was emptier than I'd expected, and quieter.

## Conditions

The sneaker-wave signs are everywhere. Rangers radio each other about tourists. Today the Atlantic had taken a day off and the water was glass. Two puffins, far out.

## Observation

The basalt columns at the east end of Reynisfjara are six-sided because the lava cooled slowly and evenly — a packing problem solved the way honeybees solve it. I stood with my hand on one of the columns for a long time. It was exactly body temperature.

A family arrived. The children immediately did what you would do: they climbed the columns like a staircase. The basalt is indifferent to us. That was the thing I came here for and didn't know it.

## Note to Self

The picture here is not the columns. It's the line of columns against the flat sand — the moment geometry meets absence.
