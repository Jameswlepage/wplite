We Run
a Room.

Elsewhere opened in 2017 in a former metal shop on Johnson Avenue. Four rooms, two bars, one rooftop, no headliner too small. We are a nonprofit, mission-led venue built by a team of musicians, promoters, and lifelong show-goers who got tired of good rooms closing.

We book across genres — punk and rap, techno and folk, experimental and pop — and we trust audiences to follow a great booking into a room they'd never normally visit. About a third of our programming is free or pay-what-you-can. A third is dedicated to artists performing in New York for the first time. The rest keeps the lights on.
